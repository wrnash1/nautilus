# Nautilus - Dive Shop Management System

Enterprise-level dive shop management software with comprehensive features for retail, courses, trips, rentals, and more.

## Features

- **Point of Sale** - Complete POS system with barcode scanning, multiple payment methods
- **Customer Management** - Full CRM with travel info, certifications, tags, and relationship tracking
- **Course Management** - Automated enrollment workflows, requirement tracking, visual rosters
- **Trip Management** - Book and manage dive trips with full payment tracking
- **Rental Equipment** - Reservation system with check-out/check-in tracking
- **Work Orders** - Repair ticket management with status tracking
- **Inventory** - Stock management with barcode support and vendor tracking
- **Cash Drawer** - Complete cash management with bill/coin counting and variance tracking
- **Reports** - Sales, customer, product, and financial reports
- **Integrations** - QuickBooks, Wave, Google Workspace, PADI/SSI certification APIs
